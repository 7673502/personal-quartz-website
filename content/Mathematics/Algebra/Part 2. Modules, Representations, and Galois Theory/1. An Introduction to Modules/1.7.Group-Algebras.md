For a field $k$ and finite group $G=\{g_1,\dots,g_n\}$, the **group-algebra** $k[G]$ consists of linear combinations of the elements of $G$. In other words, $$k[G]=\left\{\sum_{i=1}^nc_ig_i\,\Bigg|\,c_i\in k\right\}.$$
